## Reviewer notes

Ultimate Golf Community is a golf networking app focused on tee times, groups, connections, and player activity.

Core features available in this build:

- Create an account and sign in
- Build a golfer profile
- Post tee times and view open rounds
- Create and join groups
- Add golfers as connections
- Invite connections into groups
- Upload profile and group imagery

Important review notes:

1. This app uses account-based features, so sign in is required for the main experience.
2. A privacy policy is available at:
   - [https://www.ultimategolfcommunity.com/privacy](https://www.ultimategolfcommunity.com/privacy)
3. Support is available at:
   - support@ultimategolfcommunity.com
4. Account deletion is available from the Profile screen inside the app.
5. Some features are social in nature and depend on other users existing in the test environment, such as:
   - connections
   - group invites
   - messages
   - tee time participation
